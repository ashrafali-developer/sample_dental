Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about our dental services.

What are your clinic timings?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:30 AM to 8:00 PM, and on Sundays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Appointments can be booked online or by phone.
Do you accept dental insurance?
Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card during your visit, and our team will assist you with the claims process.
Is root canal treatment painful?
Not at Dental Clinic! We use advanced rotary endodontics and local anesthesia to ensure a virtually painless experience. Most patients compare it to getting a routine filling.
How much does a dental implant cost?
The cost varies depending on the case. We offer a free consultation where our implantologist will evaluate your needs and provide a transparent quote. We also have EMI options.
Do you treat children?
Absolutely. Our pediatric dentist, Dr. Priya, specializes in gentle care for kids. We make every visit fun and stress‑free for little smiles.
